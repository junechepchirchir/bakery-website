About Us

Our Story

Chichi's Cream and Cookies was founded in 2018 by June Maritim, a passionate baker who grew up watching her grandmother make homemade cookies every Sunday in their Nairobi home.

What started as a small home kitchen business quickly grew into a beloved bakery serving hundreds of customers across Nairobi. Every product is made with love, fresh ingredients, and a whole lot of butter.

Founded in 2018

Started from a home kitchen in Westlands, Nairobi.
